ARGENTINA HAS THE MOST VARIOUS DESTINATIONS THAT YOU CAN IMAGINE IN A SINGLE COUNTRY.

WHAT KINDS OF TOURIST ACTIVITIES CAN WE DO IN ARGENTINA:

  1. Cultural tourism:
    when educational activities are prioritized, such as visits to museums, cultural centers, monuments, special trains.
  2. Recreational tourism:
    for entertainment purposes, includes trips to traditional tourist places such as beaches, casinos, theme parks.
  3. Adventure or ecological tourism:
    when physical activities that can be done in natural environments predominate.
  4. Alternative tourism:
    it is non-traditional tourism, which includes activities in little frequented places.
  5. Health tourism:
    it is related to alternative therapies, such as hot springs.
